'Overview'

Dental implant surgery affects both the physical and emotional experience of the patient. As medical technology advances, operating rooms will transform, and traditional elements like dental chairs, procedures, and doctor-patient interactions will evolve. This design aims to help patients better adapt to these new technologies.

'Tech Powered Medicine'

Robotic-Assisted Healthcare in the Era of High Digitization. The involvement of implant robots in surgeries offers significant advantages. Robotic technology provides reliable and consistent intraoperative support for surgeons and surgical teams. Robotic surgical assistants can be seen as an extension of the surgeon, acting as a "hand" during surgery — one that never tires and never deviates from its position.
