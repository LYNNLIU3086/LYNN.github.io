Source of Inspiration

Physical Interaction Design

Interactive process

This technology combines micro-display screens with the refractive principles of lenses, encapsulating the internal components within the interlayer space of a spherical lens to achieve 3D display effects in compact devices.The micro-screen provides high-resolution images, while the lens focuses the refracted light into the user's eyes, creating a clear and immersive thre dimensional visual experience.

Conceptual model

The final display process offers an abstract representation of the interaction between the gut, breathing, and mental activity. The structure of the virtual gut evolves and adapts in real time, following the user's breath. The movement and changes of the virtual colonies reflect the indirect impact on respiratory health, providing a visual representation of the gut's response to different breathing patterns.
