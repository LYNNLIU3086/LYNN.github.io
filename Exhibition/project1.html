Introduction

This project integrates virtual reality (VR) technology t o provide a unique interactive experience. Viewers are immersed in key moments from the lives of self-combed women, simulating their daily routines and significant events. Through interaction, the audience becomes part of the story, experiencing this historical and cultural narrative from a first-person perspective. The VR experience breaks the limits of physical space, fostering a deeper emotional connection with the portrayed experiences.

I recreated a space that is almost a one-to-one replica of the self-shaving women's living environment, using realistic spatial layouts. Through documentary-style imagery, I present the daily lives of these women. Not only do I showcase their life stories through still images, but I also amplify the real spaces they lived in, breaking free from traditional display constraints.

By altering the scale of these spaces, I aim to create a more intimate experience, deepening the emotional connection between the audience and the content. I invite the audience into these private spaces, blurring the line between reality and representation. Surrounded by this environment, the audience connects more deeply with the experiences of self-shaving women.

Additionally, while an individual is experiencing the VR environment, the audience is also able t o observe her perspective, further enhancing the sense of immersion and empathy.
