Acupoint

Keywords: Machine Learning: Object Detection (Yolov8), Future Wearables Medium,Rhino

This study uses the YOLOv8 deep learning model to detect and locate acupuncture points along the meridians. By training on annotated images, the Al system can identify meridians on the human body and display corresponding points in real-time. Initially focused on the face and head-neck r egion, it aims to expand to full-body recognition.

Recently, artificial intelligence (Al) has been widely used in healthcare tohelp doctors diagnose diseases and create treatment plans by analyzingdata. Research is now exploring Al's role in TCM, particularly in digitizingacupoints. Al can processlarge amounts of TCM data to better understand how they work andimprove treatments. For example, deep learning can identify effectiveacupoint combinations, and image processing can detect changes in themeridians. However, challenges remain in translating TCM theories into data Al can use and overcome cultural and technical barriers.

This research aims to explore how Al can enhance the application of traditional Chinese medicine in meridian and acupoint studies. Through Al technology, we can gain a better understanding of and apply TCM, modernize its theories, and increase its influence in the global medical system.
